Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken tikka masala made easy with this great-tasting recipe â€” simply marinate chicken breast in yogurt and spices and then simmer in a tomato cream sauce. Serve with rice or warm pita bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoongits paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, 2 teaspoons cumin, coriander, cayenne, black pepper, ginger, and 3/4 teaspoon salt. Stir in chicken, cover, and refrigerate for 1 hour.
  2. Preheat a grill for high heat. Lightly oil the grill grate. Thread chicken onto skewers, and discard marinade. Grill until juices run clear, about 5 minutes on each side.
  3. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Saute garlic and jalapeno for 1 minute. Season with 2 teaspoons cumin, paprika, and 1 teaspoon salt. Stir in tomato sauce and cream. Simmer on low heat until sauce thickens, about 20 minutes. Add grilled chicken, and simmer for 10 minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
